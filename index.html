Continuous Website Development

Bad first impression = no new customer. Slow website loading times = high bounce rate. Having low quality content on your website = loack of trust in brand and products/services. Make sure you keep an open mind, always testing your website, make it easy for the customer to find what they're looking for and understand where your customer spends most of their time i.e. mobile.

Generating Leads

Outside of cold calling, email marketing, and networking events, social media, digital marketing and now the metaverse are also other ways of bringing in new customers. If you don't know who your webswite visitors are, you're missing out on opportunities on a silver platter.

Prospect Information

Living in a data-driven world, having the insights and understanding how your prospects interact with your business is important. The more context your salesperson has, the more understanding they will have if your service meets their needs. What pages of your proposal did they spend the most time on?

Sales Library

For small and medium business owners, a lot of the time sales and marketing are seen as separate functions. But now, “touching base” or “dropping in” doesn't work as well as it used to. Prospects are busier now more than ever. Without marketing content keeping your business at top of mind, and a quick, easy to access library of articles, information, statistics, comparisons, you're leaving the door wide open for your competitors to fill the gap.

Forget the 4 P's - Here Comes the 4E's

Engagement, Experience, Exclusivity and Emotion. People don't buy products - they buy emotion. Emotional branding is what gets your customer to click the Buy button. Why do you buy Apple products? Yes, the products are user friendly, and efficient for everyday use. But why not a cheaper brand? Because Apple sells exclusivity, status, and a challenge to the status quo.

AI & MarTech

Customers expect a personalized, convenient experience. And this expectation only grows day by day. Consumers these days expect brands to respond do their emotional needs, contribute to local and international communities, stand for a cause, and support great ideas. MarTech (marketing & technology), using AI, is the only and the most effective tool to gather information about customers, while meeting these ever growing needs.

Media Monitoring & Analytics

Majority of conversations about businesses, brands and their products happen online. In order to understand the existing and new customers, monitoring and understanding these key insights can help businesses improve strategy, brand reputation management, competitor analysis, market research, customer feedback, or trend forecasting.

Build 1-on-1 relations - but don't be superficial

As humans and comsumers, we are emotional and we love experiences. The real engagement will not come from a Facebook contest in which users get prizes for likes. It's an investment not worth the effort. It will bring you only short-term effects. Your follower base, your likes might increase, but they won't stay if you don't engage them on an emotional level.
